Real Time Collaboration

Google Docs and email do not work well for remotely teaching code. They don't preserve whitespace, the fonts aren't monospaced, the spell check gets in the way, etc. Paste bins are better, but they are too static if you're looking for real time teaching. W2C is what you need - a good editor and real-time collaboration with your professor.
